Might Thanes Songs

Mægenþegnasaga Fitt XIV: The Memory of First War Haldric receives visions of the primordial battle between Mategwas and Niddhogg Þā wæs Haldric hālig cyning, on his heartan hūse, heofones mægnes, mihtig on mōde. Þā him cōm swefnes cræft, swīðe dēope, fram his fæder frēan, fyrngewinnes sōð. Ġesāh hē þā on slǣpe slīðne campan, hū æt fruman fyrst feohtende wǣron Mategwas se mǣra and se morðwyrm, Nīðhogg se nāðsceaða, niðgrimm weorc. Þā wæs middangeard mǣst ungescepen, būtan blācne grund and beorhte tungol. Nīðhogg on nēolum niðer creōpende, sceaða scearpnægla, scūrheard draca. Wolde hē worlde wrāðe gescieðan, ealles ordsceafte ende macian. Ac se Mategwas, mǣgenþrym hæfde, waldend weorc-cræftes, wīsdōm and miht. "Nū sceal ūser ungemǣte feoht wesan geworht," Mategwas cwaþ. "Þū wilt, wyrmsceaða, woruld forspillan. Ic sceal foldan fram þē freoðian, lēoht fram lygesearwum, lif fram deaðe. Stande wē nū on stedefæste campstōwe, cēne beadurincas!" Fitt XV: The Shaping of Earth-Middle The cosmic battle creates the foundations of the world Þā gefeaht fyrst fēond wið frēond, heardra gehwæðer heorugrimme. Mategwas mid mǣgene moldgrundes wearð, scōp hē scearpne scield of stānum, beorgas tō bordwælle, brāde dūnscrafu. Nīðhogg mid næglum neowle dælde, clawde sē clūstertēðe ceald wæterstreamas, dēope dalu, dimme holtas. Swā wrǣttlice weorold gescēape, þurh heora heardum hilderæsum. Hwǣr se Mategwas mōdige stōd, þǣr weox wāstmas, wudubearwas grēne. Hwǣr se Nīðhogg niðer stāpode, þǣr wearð wāsten, wintrig and ceald. Tungol getācnodon tīrfæstre stōwe, hwǣr heora heardum handum gefeahton. Seofon dagas lang sige-lēas feoht, seofon nihta rīm nān oferswīðde. Þā æt nēhstan nīða heard-ost, Mategwas mōdig mǣl gespræce: "Þū scealt, slǣht-wyrm, slǣpe bindan, oð dōmesdæges dǣda gehȳrred. Ic þē on neowelnysse niðer sendan wille, under eorðgrundes innoste rōtas!" Fitt XVI: The Binding of the Serpent Mategwas chains Niddhogg beneath the World-Tree's roots Mid mihtegum wordum, Mategwas cwæð galdor and gēastas, grimme beadurūnas. Eorðe onēatne, īsenclammas, bunden of beadusearwum, brōhte tō wyrme. Fēter-clammas fæste, fyrnwit bunden, ymbhwyrft handum heardne scynscaðan. Nīðhogg ne mihte nōwiht rīsan, būtan rōta under rynum gnagan. "Hēr scealt þū habban," Mategwas sæġde, "hūs under hrusan, heardne slǣpe. Þonne middangeard mǣst geblīcað, and folc fǣringa fyrnrēht forlǣteð, þonne meaht þū mǣþum morðor macian. Ac oð þām daege, dēopestr slǣpe!" Gewāt þā se godfruma goldweard, scīmende scyppend scīre tungl, mōnan and mearce, middangeardes weard. Eft tō his bearnum blis āsende, līf and lustfulnes, lēoht and hāle. Hālige gāstas ofer hrōfas sendan, bletsigan foldan bearnum mannum, ēce miltse eall tōgædre. Swā gefrēodode frēa ælmihtig weorold fram wīte, wuldor gefēode. Fitt XVII: The Awakening and the Oath-Swearing Haldric awakens from the vision and gathers his first oath-sworn companions Þā onwōc se cyning craft-wīs Haldric, fram þām swefne swīðum, sōðcyn wita. Ealne æþelan ellen oncnēow, hū his fæder fæder feoht gewann, woruld geworhte wīsdōme þurh, freoðe foldan fram feondsceaðan. Nū hē mihte māran mōd oncnāwan, hwæt him gedafenað dēdum tō wyrcenne. Gegyrede hine gearo goldhringe cyning, nam his handgesweord, hlūd horn blēow. Tō him cōmon cumlīðe cēne beadurincas, þegnas on þēawum, þrīdda dǣl eallra æþelinga þe on earde wǣron, sōhton hine sylfne under sunnan scīman. "Hwæt, gē gōdfruman!" Haldric him tō cwæð, "Wille gē wið mē wearþan tō sīðe?" "Þū eart ūser cyning, cnihtwesende!" Sægde se forma folces lǣttēow. "Wē willað mid þē weorodes menigan, faran tō feorrlande, feohtende beadu, winnende wuldor, weorðmynde gestrīenan!" Þā swōron hīe ealle āðas fæste, trēowðe getācnodon, tīrfæstre gecȳðu, oð deaðes daeg drōhtnes þegnas. Fitt XVIII: The First Hall-Building Haldric establishes his royal seat and the great mead-hall Þā sōhte se cyning cēne beaduwiga, stōwe on staþole stearce getimbrode, healle healice, hlūdne þrymseld. Gecēas hē cyneburh on cnolle hēahum, þǣr æt fruman fyrst folde grēttest, beorgas ymbūtan beorhte stānweallas, wæterstrēamas wlitebeorhte flōwon. "Hēr sceal hālig ham healdend wesan," Haldric hēahcyning hlūde sæġde, "māran meduhealle mōnna bearnum þonne æfre ǣr eorðbūende geworhton under wolcnum, wuldorprym micel!" Swā het hē his þegnas þearflīce wyrcean, timbran trumne torht cyneseld, goldgerenode glæmstānas settan. Seofon mōnað lang manfulne weorc, dæges and nihtes deorlīce timbran. Āhōfon hēah hrōf, hlūdne gyldenheall, mearc-stānas mǣre around medubenċum. Ealdgereht eall innanwearde, beāgas and byrnan, beorhte frætwe, wǣpna gehwilc weorðfullne stōd, gearo tō gūðe goldhroden cyning. Þā wæs heall gearo, hlūd under heofone, mārost meduþegnas meþelstede fundon. Gecwǣdon cyning cēne Haldrice: "Hwæt sceal hātan hūs þis māre?" Ða ondswarede æþeling gōd: "Þunresheal sceal hātan þegnseld þis! Hēr sceal hūnigswēte hlūde flotian, beorhtast beadurinc blisse habban!" Fitt XIX: The Shadow in the North News comes of strange happenings in the northern wastelands Þā wæs on Þunresheale þegn-sibb micel, bliss and beorscipe, beadurinc manig æt symbeldæge sibb gefremede. Þǣr wæs hearpan swēg, hlēoðor wrǣttlic, glee-manna sang geond goldwearde, word-wyrhtan cræft wīsdōm-berend sealde soðlēoð under seld-hrōfe. Ac þā cōm cuma ceald of norðan, hrēowmod hæleð hūru spræce: "Hēr sindon tīðinga torne gehladene! On norðwegas nīðgrim weorc, eorðan æfre īdelnes wexeð. Folde forsitteð, frumbearn sweltað, næfre nǣnig nīht ne dæg sunnan scīman sōðfæst beleohteð." "Hwæt is þāra tacna?" Haldric frægn, cyning cēne cwic andsware. "Blāce beorgas beorhte næfre, deorcnyss dæghwamlīce dimmer weaxeð. Ealne weg wyrmfāh wīde spongon, sweart swāðu swearte nǣddran, Nīðhogges cyn niðer crēopende, fēond-scafan fela foldan spilgað!" Þā āstōd se æþeling, eal-wealda helm, grīmhȳdig guma gūð-bil genam. "Nū is tīma cumen tīrfæst weorc, þæt wē norðweard niðe sōhton! Ne mæg ic munduclic mīnum lēodum lǣtan līðan lāðra fēonda nīð ne nearowe, nefne ic norðan fare!" Fitt XX: The Gathering of the War-Host Haldric marshals his army to march north against the growing darkness Þā sōna æfter sund-buendum horn gehīerde hlūde blāwan, geond eall ēþel æþelinges bōd: "Cumað, campgearwe cēne rincas! Tīma is tō farenne tō feoht-stōwe, wīgend wiðstandan wyrmcynn fāh, beaduwerk beornan būtan ōþrum!" Cōmon þā cēne of cyneburge, þrēo þūsenda þegna gehwilc, byrnum gebyrged, billum tō gearowe. Brunfāg bunden bord-weall fæst, æscolt and eccg eall gebeorht, helm and hring-byrne hlūde scīnende, campweoroda cyning cūð and mǣre. Þǣm fyrdweorode fæger siððan Haldric cyning hlūde mǣlde: "Gē sindon gōde gārsecge menn, cēne campweorude cyning-weorðe! Nū sceal ūser ellen æþelinges weorðan, feoht-grādig faran folce tō helpe, lǣtan linde weorðan hlūde ymb handa, grimme gāras geond grǣsland fleogan!" "Gif wē dēað drihtnes dǣdum moton, in Wulderhalle weorðian sittan, giefan us godfyrht glēde niman! Ac gif wē libban lȳtel hwīle, sige-tīr strīenan under sunnan scīman, þonne bið ūser noma nihtlanges heard on scopes sange scīran gehīered!" Hlōhan þā beadurinc, bil-swīðe wer, gearwe tō gange goldwine folces. Fitt XXI: The March to Shadow-Realm The war-band travels north into increasingly desolate and cursed lands Gewāt þā ofer geofon goldwine folces, mōdig mægenþegn magas lǣdan, norðwearde on nihtscūa land, þǣr næfre nǣnig nihtegale sang, ne blōstma ne blæd beorhte ne grēow, eall wæs andsware īdel and wēste. Þrīe dagas lang þēgn-weoroda mæst fērde forð on weg folce gelǣdde, oð þæt hīe gesāwon sārig tacen: hrīmge heafodbeorg hlūde stōd, þanon cōm mist morgen-ceald, deorcfāh nēol dimhȳdig sceadu ymbhwyrft swīðe sweart and heard. "Hwæt is þes hlāw?" Haldric frægn, se þe mōst meahte mǣrne gūð-rinc. Andswearede eald gūð-beorn: "Þis is se stōw stæðolfæst ǣr, þǣr æt fruman fyrst folde wearð geworht wið wyrmum wīsdōme þurh. Hēr bindeð æfre bealu-rūnas Nīðhogg neðan, under neowolnysse." "Ac nū sindon slǣpe-bendas heardan handum hēr tōslitene! Lōciað, lēofe men, hū ligetu springað fram foldan sceat fȳr-drācena! Wyrmfāh wīde wrixle fleogað, Nīðhogges bearn nihtscaðu macaþ!" Þā ondrǣdde duguð and geogoð, ac Haldric cyning heort ne forhte. Fitt XXII: The Final Battle and the Binding Renewed Þā gestōd se cyning cēne on campstōwe, Haldric se hēahgōda hlūde mǣlde: "Nū is endedæg eallra gesceafta! Hēr sceal weorðan wīg ōðer twēga: oððe middangeard morðe spilgan, oððe freoðu findan folce tō helpe!" Þā āstāh of eorðan egeslīc wyrm, Nīðhogg se nāðscaða nihtscūan lǣdde. Sweart wæs his scūa, scearpan tēðum, īsenclammas ealle tōbrocen, bindrūnas gebāne on foldan. Spræc þā se slīðgrim sweartan reorde: "Ic eom æþeling ealdra dagas, ǣr þonne tungol tīd ġerīmdon! Hwæt wenest þū, wǣpnes bearhtme, tōgēanes mē tō campian? Ic eom deāð and dimnes, dēop under eorðan, ic eom ende ealra þinga!" Haldric him andswarede, æþeling gōd: "Þū eart lȳgend on langan tīde! Ic eom lēoht and līf, lāst Mategwases. Þurh mē sceal middangeard miltse findan, folc under heofone freoðu habban. Stande nū, scynscaða, tō slege gearo!" Þā gegripon hīe grimmum handum, cyning and wyrmsceaða campstōwe on. Beorhtast beadugār bill-swīðne Haldric āhōf hlūdre stefne. Nīðhogg mid næglum niðer clawode, wunde geworhte wīgfruman on. Seofon tīda lang tōgēanes fuhton, þæt eall middangeard mearc oncnēow. Dūnscrafas dyneden, dēope sǣstreamas wrixlodon on waðum wīde geond eorðan. Tungol teotrodon, tīd āblended, swā heardum hilte hīe campoden. Þā æt nīehstan nīðgrimm wyrm fēoll on foldan, fēorh āswāpte. Haldric se hālga hlūde sæġde, clypode tō heofone cēne wordum: "Fæder, þū mihtiġa! Gefrēoda nū foldan fram þissum feondsceaðan, bind hine fæste under eorðbyrġenne, oð ende worlde!" Þā cōmon englas of ūplīfte, hæfdon īsenclammas hāte ġesmīðode. Bundon þone bealofullan under beorgscræfum, setton seofan siġel ofer sārige stōwe, þæt hē nǣfre eft ne ārīse, oð dōmesdægas dēope slǣpe. Gewāt þā goldwine gōdum geclǣnsod, Haldric tō heofonum hlūdre stefne: "Nū is freoðu geworht folce eallu! Wuniaþ on wynne, weoroda mǣste! Ic fare tō fæder, freoðuwebbe wesan, ac mīn gāst wunað mid gōdum mannum!" Swā wearð āfylled fyrngewritu, Cantica Antiqua cēne geendod. Bidde ic ealle þā þis gehȳrað: gemunað mǣrða Mategwases, and Haldrices hālige dǣda, þæt gē mōtan miltse findan on ēcum līfe æþelan cyninges! Fitt XXIII: The Throne-Gift and the Hall's Glory The immediate aftermath of Haldric's victory and his final royal acts Þā cōmon campwīgan cēne tō cyninge, þēgnas tō þrymsetle þanc-hycgende. Gesāwon hīe sigores swēgle beacen, hū se hālga cyning heofone nēalǣhte. Ac ǣr hē gewāt tō wuldres selde, spræc hē tō his folce fæger wordum: "Nū sceal cynerīce cræft habban! Ic eow lǣfe lāre mīne, rīht and rǣdas rūm-heorte, þæt gē mōton mihte weorðian oð ende daga ēce sibbe." Gesette hē þā goldne cynehelm on his mǣgoðegnes mǣrne heafod, Ælfgār se æþele, eorla lēofost. "Þū scealt rīce rǣdan georne, folc on freoðu fæste healdan, oð þæt cume cyning nīwe!" Gewāt þā goldwine gǣste tō heofone, lēoht-berhtne līc lǣfde on eorðan. Bebyrigedon beornas cēne under hlǣwe hēahum hlāforde lēofne, setton sigel-mearc ofer sāre stōwe: "Hēr līð Haldric, hæleda cyning!" Fitt XXIV: The Waning of the Elder Days The passing of the golden age and the coming of darker times Monig winter wæs siððan, þæt folc wunode on freoðu lāngum. Ælfgār cyning æþele rǣdde, geheold his þēode on þǣre hālgan ǣ, oð þæt hē ealdode, ellen wanode. Þā cōmon tīðas torne geblonden: beorn æfter beorne burh forlēton, sōhton sǣstrēamas sundor lande. Næfre siððan swā micel þrym under Þunres-heale þegnas gesǣton, ac tōglīdon tīrfæste dagas. Hwīlum hīe gemundon mǣrra dǣda, þonne scop-crǣftig scīran lēoðe sang æt symble soðgyddinga ymbe Haldrices hēahþē dǣda. Þonne wǣron weras blīðe, gemunon mǣgenþrymm Mategwases, hū hē worolde wræc ǣt fruman. Ac flōwon dagas swā flōd on sǣ, wendon geāra wīde tō lȳtle. Beorgas hnīcodon, burgweallas fēollon, menn ne curon māran ellen swā hīe on ærdagum ǣr dydon, þā Haldric cyning hlāforde folde. Fitt XXV: The Seer's Prophecy and the Thread Unbroken The final fitt, prophesying future heroes and the eternal return of light Þā wæs sum wīsa on westene, eald ǣrendgā ēðelweardas, sē þe seah forð on sēoce tīdan, hwonne cumen sceolde cyning nīwe, līcost Haldrice on lāngum fyrste. Spræc hē þā tō folce fæger gyddinga: "Ne bēoð þās dagas dēaðe endelaas! Ic geseah on sweofnum swutol tacen: beacen blīcan ofer beorgscrafum, nīwe tungol norðan cuman, cyninges beacen cūð and mǣre! Þonne sceal āsprigan spēdiges cynn, beorn of beornum beorhte cyning, sē þe sceal wīð wyrme wīge stondan, Nīðhogges sunu nīwe bindan, gif hē eft arise of eorðscræfum, þurh folces synna freoðu tōbrecan!" "Geþencaþ, gūðrinc, hwonne gē gehȳraþ horn hlūdne ofer hēahbeorgum: þæt bið tācen tīrfæst cyning cymð tō eorðan cræft habban! Þonne sceal ende ealra sorga, lēoht æfter þȳstrum lixan scīne!" Swā spræc sē snottra, sōð-gefylled, and gewāt þā tō wuda dīglum.
